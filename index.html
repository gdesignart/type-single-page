Nickel Gothic

History

Nickel Gothic is a modern designed font created by David Jonathan Ross, A well known typographic designer that is known for creating fonts based off of historical ideas and making them modern. This font was particularly inspired by lettering on a 1918 Chinese banknote. Some other fonts that inspired Ross in creating this typography were Microgramma and Eurostile along with other fonts from the 70s like Neographik and Serpentine. The most defining feature of this font is the extreme flat tops and bottoms.
